CELL THEORY:
- All living things are made of cells; cells are the basic unit of life; new cells arise from existing cells
- Eukaryote vs prokaryote: nucleus, membrane-bound organelles, ribosome size (80S vs 70S), DNA organization (linear with histones vs circular nucleoid)

ORGANELLES:
- Nucleus: nuclear envelope (double membrane, continuous with RER), nuclear pores (Ran-GTP gradient, NLS/NES), nucleolus (rRNA synthesis + ribosome assembly)
- Mitochondria: double membrane, own circular DNA (maternal inheritance), matrix (TCA, beta-ox), inner membrane (ETC, ATP synthase), IMS (proton gradient), endosymbiotic theory
- Rough ER: ribosomes, secretory/membrane/lysosomal protein synthesis, SRP pathway, N-linked glycosylation begins, disulfide bonds, ERAD
- Smooth ER: lipid synthesis, steroid synthesis, cytochrome P450 detoxification, Ca2+ storage (SERCA; SR in muscle)
- Golgi: cis (receiving) -> medial -> trans (shipping); O-linked glycosylation, M6P tagging for lysosomes, sorting; constitutive vs regulated secretion
- Lysosomes: acid hydrolases (pH ~5, V-type H+ ATPase), autophagy, phagocytic digestion, receptor recycling; storage diseases (Tay-Sachs, Gaucher, I-cell)
- Peroxisomes: H2O2 metabolism (catalase), VLCFA beta-oxidation, plasmalogen synthesis; Zellweger syndrome

ENDOMEMBRANE / TRAFFICKING:
- COPII: ER -> Golgi (anterograde)
- COPI: Golgi -> ER (retrograde, KDEL retrieval)
- Clathrin: trans-Golgi -> lysosomes; PM endocytosis

CYTOSKELETON:
- Microfilaments (actin, 7nm): muscle contraction, cytokinesis (contractile ring), cell motility, microvilli; +/- ends; cytochalasin, phalloidin
- Intermediate filaments (10nm): tissue-specific (keratin epithelia, vimentin mesenchymal, desmin muscle, neurofilaments, lamins, GFAP); non-polar; tensile strength; epidermolysis bullosa
- Microtubules (alpha/beta tubulin, 25nm): mitotic spindle, intracellular transport, cilia/flagella (9+2); dynamic instability (GTP cap)
- Motors: kinesin (+ end, anterograde), dynein (- end, retrograde, axonemal in cilia); Kartagener
- Drug targets: colchicine (anti-polym), taxol (anti-depolym), vincristine, cytochalasin

PROKARYOTIC CELL STRUCTURE:
- Cell wall: peptidoglycan (NAG-NAM cross-linked); Gram+ (thick wall, teichoic acids, purple) vs Gram- (thin wall + outer membrane + LPS/endotoxin, periplasm with beta-lactamases, pink)
- Lysozyme cleaves peptidoglycan
- Ribosomes: 70S (30S+50S); antibiotic targets (aminoglycosides, tetracyclines on 30S; chloramphenicol, macrolides, linezolid on 50S); mitochondria/chloroplasts also 70S
- Flagella (flagellin, proton motive force), pili (attachment, F-pilus for conjugation), plasmids (R plasmids, virulence)
- Nucleoid region (no membrane); DNA gyrase target of fluoroquinolones; coupled transcription/translation
- Endospores: Bacillus, Clostridium; dipicolinic acid; resistant to autoclaving must use 121C/15psi/15min
- Biofilms, quorum sensing, antibiotic resistance ~1000x

PROKARYOTIC GROWTH & GENETIC TRANSFER:
- Binary fission (FtsZ ring); growth curve: lag, log, stationary, death
- Transformation: uptake of free DNA (Griffith experiment)
- Transduction: phage-mediated (generalized = random; specialized = near prophage site)
- Conjugation: F+ -> F- via sex pilus; Hfr (integrated F) transfers chromosomal genes

VIRUSES:
- Obligate intracellular parasites; no metabolism, no ribosomes
- Structure: nucleic acid (DNA/RNA, ss/ds, linear/circular, segmented) + capsid (icosahedral or helical) +/- envelope (host membrane derived)
- Baltimore classes I-VII: dsDNA, ssDNA, dsRNA, (+)ssRNA, (-)ssRNA, ssRNA-RT (HIV), dsDNA-RT (HepB)
- Lytic cycle: attachment -> penetration -> biosynthesis -> assembly -> lysis
- Lysogenic cycle: integration as prophage, repressor (cI), SOS-triggered switch to lytic; lysogenic conversion (diphtheria, botulinum toxins)
- Retroviruses: reverse transcriptase (with RNase H) -> dsDNA -> integrase -> provirus; HIV gp120-CD4 + CCR5/CXCR4; HAART targets RT, integrase, protease, fusion
- Bacteriophages: T-even (lytic), lambda (temperate); Hershey-Chase

PRIONS:
- Pure protein (no nucleic acid); PrPC (alpha-helical) -> PrPSc (beta-sheet, autocatalytic template)
- Spongiform encephalopathies: CJD, BSE, scrapie, kuru
- Resistant to autoclaving, UV, formaldehyde

FUNGI (Kaplan brief):
- Eukaryotic, heterotrophs, absorption-based
- Cell wall: chitin (vs peptidoglycan in bacteria, cellulose in plants)
- Membrane: ergosterol (drug target -- amphotericin B, azoles)
- Yeasts (budding), molds (hyphae/mycelium), dimorphic ("mold cold, yeast heat" -- Histoplasma, Blastomyces, Coccidioides)
