# BB Chapter 2 — Enzymes

Scope: Enzymes as biological catalysts, mechanisms, enzyme kinetics, effects of local conditions, regulation.

AAMC FC mapping: FC1A (Protein Structure and Function) — enzyme portion; also FC5E (CP, Michaelis-Menten kinetics)

Kaplan: Biochem Ch 2

---

ENZYME KINETICS: Primary content lives in `Content/CP/` FC5E (tested in both CP and BB). Core concepts:
- Enzymes as biological catalysts (lower activation energy, do not change ΔG)
- Mechanisms: proximity/orientation, acid-base catalysis, covalent catalysis, strain
- Michaelis-Menten: Km, Vmax, kcat
- Competitive, noncompetitive, uncompetitive, mixed inhibition (Lineweaver-Burk patterns)
- Effects of local conditions: temperature optimum, pH optimum
- Regulation:
  - Allosteric regulation (cooperativity, sigmoidal vs hyperbolic kinetics)
  - Feedback inhibition (end-product inhibits early enzyme)
  - Covalent modification (phosphorylation, proteolytic cleavage)
  - Zymogens (trypsinogen → trypsin)
  - Isozymes
