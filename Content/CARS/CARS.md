# CARS -- CRITICAL ANALYSIS AND REASONING SKILLS

53 questions, 90 minutes. 9 passages (500-600 words) with 5-7 questions each. NO outside content knowledge required.

---

## Passage Types (50/50 split)

HUMANITIES (50%): architecture, art, dance, ethics, literature, music, philosophy, popular culture, religion, theater, diverse cultural studies

SOCIAL SCIENCES (50%): anthropology, archaeology, economics, education, geography, history, linguistics, political science, population health, psychology, sociology

---

## Skills Tested

SKILL 1 -- Foundations of Comprehension (~30%):
- Main idea identification
- Specific detail recognition
- Understanding argument structure
- Inferring meaning from context
- Recognizing tone and rhetorical purpose
- Defining words in context

SKILL 2 -- Reasoning Within the Text (~30%):
- Integrating passage components
- Evaluating evidence and arguments
- Assessing credibility
- Identifying relationships (cause-effect, comparison, analogy)
- Recognizing assumptions
- Distinguishing supported vs unsupported claims

SKILL 3 -- Reasoning Beyond the Text (~40%):
- Applying passage info to new situations
- Assessing impact of new information on arguments
- Extrapolating to new contexts
- Predicting outcomes
- Evaluating strengthen/weaken evidence
- Recognizing analogous situations
- Determining author's likely response to new scenarios
