# CP — FC5D: Biologically Relevant Molecules (Orgo, Biochem)

Parent section: Foundational Concept 5 — Chemical principles govern interactions

---

AMINO ACIDS:
- Structure, classification (nonpolar, polar, positive, negative)
- alpha-carbon configuration, zwitterions, pI calculations
- Peptide bond formation/hydrolysis

PROTEINS:
- Primary through quaternary structure
- Denaturation
- Non-enzymatic function (structural, motor, immune, signaling)

NUCLEOTIDES/NUCLEIC ACIDS:
- Base pairing, DNA double helix, RNA structure
- Sugar-phosphate backbone

LIPIDS:
- Triacylglycerols, phospholipids, sphingolipids
- Steroids (cholesterol ring structure), prostaglandins
- Fatty acid structure (saturated vs unsaturated)
- Fat-soluble vitamins, saponification

CARBOHYDRATES:
- Monosaccharide classification, anomers, epimers
- Cyclic structures (Haworth projections)
- Glycosidic bonds, disaccharides, polysaccharides

ORGANIC REACTIONS TESTED:
- SN1/SN2/E1/E2 decision framework
- Aldehyde/ketone nucleophilic addition (acetal, hemiacetal, imine, enamine, cyanohydrin)
- Enolate chemistry (aldol condensation, retro-aldol)
- Alcohol reactions (oxidation, substitution)
- Carboxylic acid reactions (ester/amide formation, reduction, decarboxylation)
- Acid derivative reactions (nucleophilic acyl substitution, transesterification, hydrolysis)
- Reactivity ranking: acyl halide > anhydride > ester > amide
