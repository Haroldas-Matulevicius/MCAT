# CP — FC5C: Separation and Purification (Orgo, Biochem)

Parent section: Foundational Concept 5 — Chemical principles govern interactions

---

- Extraction (liquid-liquid, acid-base)
- Distillation (simple, fractional, vacuum)
- Chromatography: TLC (Rf values), column, HPLC, gas chromatography
- Size-exclusion chromatography (larger elute first)
- Ion-exchange chromatography
- Affinity chromatography (ligand-specific, highest purity)
- Electrophoresis: agarose gel, native PAGE, SDS-PAGE (separates by size -- SDS denatures + uniform negative charge), isoelectric focusing, 2D gel
- Centrifugation: differential, density gradient/ultracentrifugation
- Recrystallization
- Filtration, dialysis

Kaplan: Orgo Ch 12, Biochem Ch 3. Students consistently underestimate how often lab techniques appear in passages.
