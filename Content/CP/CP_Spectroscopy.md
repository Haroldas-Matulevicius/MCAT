# CP — Spectroscopy (Orgo)

---

IR SPECTROSCOPY -- key absorptions:
- Broad O-H: ~3200-3550 (alcohol), ~2500-3300 (carboxylic acid)
- Sharp C=O: ~1700-1750
- N-H: ~3300-3500 (1 peak 2nd amine, 2 peaks 1st amine)
- C-O: ~1000-1250

1H NMR:
- Chemical shift (delta, ppm), TMS reference at 0
- Shielding/deshielding
- Splitting: n+1 rule
- Integration: area proportional to number of protons
- D2O shake removes O-H and N-H peaks

13C NMR: number of unique carbon environments, no splitting

MASS SPECTROMETRY:
- Molecular ion peak (M+)
- Common fragment losses: 15 (CH3), 17 (OH), 18 (H2O), 28 (CO), 29 (CHO), 45 (OEt)
- Base peak = tallest

UV-Vis:
- Beer-Lambert law: A=epsilon*l*c
- Conjugation -> longer lambda_max
