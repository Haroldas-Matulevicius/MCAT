## Fat-Soluble Vitamins: "ADEK"
- **A** — retinoids (vision)
- **D** — calcium absorption, bone
- **E** — antioxidant (tocopherol)
- **K** — clotting factors (γ-carboxylation of II, VII, IX, X; warfarin target)

Water-soluble: everything else (Bs, C) — excreted in urine, NOT stored.

## Fatty Acid Nomenclature
- **n:d** format — n = # carbons, d = # double bonds (e.g., palmitate 16:0, oleate 18:1)
- **ω-3, ω-6** — first double bond counted from methyl end
- **"Omega-3s from fish"** — EPA, DHA

## Saturated vs Unsaturated (Packing)
- **Saturated** → straight chain → packs tight → solid at room T (butter)
- **Cis unsaturated** → KINK → loose packing → liquid at room T (oils)
- **Trans unsaturated** → straight (no kink) → packs like saturated → CV risk
- "Cis Kinks, Trans Straight"

## Eicosanoids (20C PUFAs)
- **COX** → **prostaglandins + thromboxanes** (inflammation, platelets; NSAIDs inhibit)
- **LOX** → **leukotrienes** (asthma)

## Essential Fatty Acids (cannot synthesize)
- **Linoleic** (ω-6) → arachidonic acid → eicosanoids
- **α-Linolenic** (ω-3)

## Cholesterol Rings
- "**4 rings: 3 six + 1 five**" — steroid nucleus
- Precursor to: bile salts, steroid hormones, vitamin D
