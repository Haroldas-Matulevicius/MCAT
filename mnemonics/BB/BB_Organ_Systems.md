# Organ Systems Mnemonics

## Heart Valves: "LAB RAT" + "Try Pulling My Aorta"
- **LAB RAT**: **L**eft Atrium = **B**icuspid (mitral); **R**ight Atrium = **T**ricuspid
- Blood flow order: **T**ricuspid -> **P**ulmonary -> **M**itral -> **A**ortic
- Full path: Vena cava -> RA -> Tricuspid -> RV -> Pulmonary valve -> Lungs -> Pulmonary veins -> LA -> Mitral -> LV -> Aortic valve -> Aorta

---

## WBC Types by Abundance: "Never Let Monkeys Eat Bananas"
- **N**eutrophils (~60-70%): first responders, phagocytes, innate immunity
- **L**ymphocytes (~25-33%): T cells, B cells, NK cells; adaptive immunity
- **M**onocytes (~4-8%): become macrophages/dendritic cells in tissues
- **E**osinophils (~1-4%): parasites, allergies
- **B**asophils (~0.5-1%): least abundant, release histamine

---

## T Cell / MHC Matching: "Rule of 8"
- CD**4** x MHC **II** = 8 (Helper T cells recognize MHC class II)
- CD**8** x MHC **I** = 8 (Cytotoxic T cells recognize MHC class I)
- MHC I = ALL nucleated cells (intracellular antigens)
- MHC II = APCs only (dendritic cells, macrophages, B cells)

---

## Antibody Classes: "GAMED"
- **IgG**: most abundant (~75%), crosses placenta, secondary response, opsonization
- **IgA**: dimer, mucosal secretions (saliva, tears, breast milk). "A for Alimentary"
- **IgM**: pentamer, FIRST antibody produced, best at complement fixation, too big to cross placenta
- **IgE**: allergies + parasites, binds mast cells/basophils -> histamine release
- **IgD**: B-cell receptor on surface, role in B-cell activation

---

## Nephron Flow: "Bowman's -> PCT -> Loop -> DCT -> Collecting Duct"

### Loop of Henle: "Descending = Water; Ascending = Salt"
- **D**escending limb: permeable to water (water Drops down), impermeable to NaCl
- **A**scending limb: permeable to salt (salt Ascends out), impermeable to water
- Creates the countercurrent multiplier system

---

## Small Intestine: "Dow Jones Industrial" (DJI)
- **D**uodenum: receives bile + pancreatic enzymes, most chemical digestion
- **J**ejunum: primary nutrient absorption
- **I**leum: absorbs vitamin B12 + bile salts, Peyer's patches (lymphoid tissue)

---

## Duodenal Proteases: "Eat Tender Chicken Chunks Elegantly"
- **E**nterokinase: activates trypsinogen -> trypsin (from intestinal brush border)
- **T**rypsin: cleaves at basic residues (Arg, Lys); activates other zymogens
- **C**hymotrypsin: cleaves at hydrophobic residues
- **C**arboxypeptidase: cleaves from C-terminus
- **E**lastase: cleaves at small, nonpolar residues

---

## GI Hormones: "Garlic Cloves Smell Exquisite"
- **G**astrin (G cells, stomach): stimulates HCl + pepsinogen
- **C**CK (I cells, duodenum): gallbladder contraction, pancreatic enzymes, inhibits gastric emptying
- **S**ecretin (S cells, duodenum): pancreatic bicarbonate secretion (neutralize acid)
- **E**nterogastrone/GIP (K cells, duodenum): inhibits gastric acid, stimulates insulin

---

## Sarcomere Bands: "HI Shorten, A Stays"
- **H** zone (thick filaments only) = SHORTENS during contraction
- **I** band (thin filaments only) = SHORTENS during contraction
- **A** band (full length of thick filaments) = does NOT change
- "H is fat like thick filaments, I is skinny like thin filaments"
- Z-lines move closer together (overall sarcomere shortens)

### Thin Filament Components: "AT&T"
- **A**ctin + **T**roponin + **T**ropomyosin
- Ca2+ binds troponin C -> tropomyosin shifts -> myosin binds actin -> cross-bridge cycling

---

## Blood Typing: "You make antibodies against what you DON'T have"
| Type | Antigens | Antibodies |
|------|----------|------------|
| A | A | Anti-B |
| B | B | Anti-A |
| AB | A and B | None (universal recipient) |
| O | None | Anti-A and Anti-B (universal donor) |

---

## Respiratory Volumes
- 4 Volumes: TV (~0.5L), IRV (~3.0L), ERV (~1.0L), RV (~1.2L)
- 4 Capacities:
  - IC = TV + IRV
  - VC = TV + IRV + ERV (max air in one breath)
  - FRC = ERV + RV (air after normal exhale)
  - TLC = TV + IRV + ERV + RV (~6L, everything)
- RV = air that ALWAYS stays in lungs (can't be measured by spirometry)
