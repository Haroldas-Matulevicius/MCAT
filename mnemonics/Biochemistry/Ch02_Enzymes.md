## Inhibition Patterns on Lineweaver-Burk — "CUP Mixed"

- **Competitive**: Vmax unchanged, Km ↑ (lines cross at y-axis). "Compete for the active site; outcompete with more substrate."
- **Uncompetitive**: Vmax ↓, Km ↓ (parallel lines). "Un-common — only binds the ES complex."
- **Pure Noncompetitive**: Vmax ↓, Km unchanged (lines cross at x-axis). "Non-anywhere — binds E or ES equally."
- **Mixed**: Vmax ↓, Km shifts (varies; cross upper-left quadrant).

## Enzyme Regulation Quick Check

- **Phosphorylation ON / Phosphorylation OFF** — most enzymes follow glycogen phosphorylase rule (ON when phosphorylated). EXCEPTION: PDC (ON when **dephosphorylated**) and glycogen synthase (ON when dephosphorylated).
  - Rule: storage enzymes (glycogen synthase) = ON when dephos; breakdown enzymes (phosphorylase) = ON when phos.

## Catalytic Strategies
