# BB Chapter 4 — Carbohydrates — Mnemonics

Scope: Classification, stereochemistry, polysaccharides, reducing sugars.

## Aldose vs Ketose
- **Ald**ose = **ald**ehyde (C=O at C1)
- **Ket**ose = **ket**one (C=O usually at C2; e.g., fructose)

## D vs L
- **D**-sugar = OH on **D**extra (**D** = right) of farthest chiral center in Fischer
- All biological sugars are D (mirror-image rule: L-amino acids, D-sugars)

## Epimer vs Anomer vs Enantiomer
- **Enantiomers**: mirror images (differ at ALL chiral centers) — D-glucose vs L-glucose
- **Diastereomers**: differ at ≥1 but not all centers
- **Epimers**: differ at EXACTLY ONE center (subset of diastereomers) — glucose/galactose (C4), glucose/mannose (C2)
- **Anomers**: special epimers at the **anomeric carbon** (former carbonyl, C1 in hexoses) — α vs β

## α vs β Anomer
- In D-sugar Haworth projection: **α** = OH **D**own (mnemonic: **α-D-own**); **β** = OH **U**p (**β-U-p**)

## Polysaccharide Linkages
- **Starch** (plant) = α-1,4 (+ α-1,6 branches in amylopectin)
- **Glycogen** (animal) = α-1,4 + α-1,6 (MORE branched than starch)
- **Cellulose** (plant wall) = β-1,4 (humans can't digest)
- **Chitin** (fungi/insects) = β-1,4 NAG

Rule: α = digestible by humans; β = fiber (except lactose β-1,4 is broken by lactase)

## Reducing Sugars
- **All monosaccharides** are reducing (free anomeric C)
- **Disaccharides**: maltose, lactose = reducing; **sucrose = NON-reducing** (both anomeric Cs tied up)
- Tests: Benedict's, Tollens', Fehling's (all positive on reducing sugars)
