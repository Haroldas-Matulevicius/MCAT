## Purines vs. Pyrimidines
- **"CUT the PY"** = Pyrimidines are **C**ytosine, **U**racil, **T**hymine (1 ring, smaller)
- **"Pure As Gold"** = Purines are **A**denine, **G**uanine (2 rings, larger)
- Counter-intuitive: pyrimidine is a longer word but has fewer rings
- Base pairing: A-T (2 H-bonds), G-C (3 H-bonds). In RNA: A-U

## DNA Replication Enzymes: "Happy Teens Party Partying Late"
- **H**elicase (unwind) -> **T**opoisomerase/SSBs (relieve supercoiling, protect) -> **P**rimase (lay RNA primer) -> DNA **P**ol III (synthesize, 5'->3', proofreads 3'->5') -> DNA Pol I (remove primers, fill gaps) -> **L**igase (seal nicks between Okazaki fragments)

## Direction Rules
- All DNA pol = **5'→3' synthesis only** (proofreads 3'→5')
- "Synthesis only South (5→3); proofread North (3→5)"

## DNA Repair
- UV damage → thymine dimers → **NER** ("Nucleotide = big patch, like a bike tire")
- Small base damage → **BER** ("Base = single swap")
- Replication slip-through → **MMR** ("MisMatch")

## Blotting: "SNoW DRoP"
- **S**outhern = **D**NA
- **N**orthern = **R**NA
- **W**estern = **P**rotein
- Southern named after Edwin Southern; Northern and Western are compass-direction puns

## Gel Electrophoresis: "Small Runs Far" + "Run to Red"
- Smaller fragments migrate faster/farther
- DNA is negatively charged (phosphate backbone) -> runs toward positive electrode (anode, red)
- SDS-PAGE: SDS gives all proteins uniform negative charge -> separation by size only

## PCR Steps: "DAE" at 95-55-72
- **D**enature (~95C): separate strands
- **A**nneal (~55C): primers bind
- **E**xtend (~72C): Taq polymerase synthesizes
- Temperatures go DOWN then UP (valley shape)
- 2^n copies after n cycles

## RT-PCR vs qPCR
- **RT**-PCR = **R**everse **T**ranscription (mRNA → cDNA), then PCR
- **q**PCR = **q**uantitative, real-time
- Trap: "RT" is NOT "real-time"

## Sanger Sequencing
- "**dd**NTPs = **D**one, **D**one" (no 3'-OH → chain terminates)
