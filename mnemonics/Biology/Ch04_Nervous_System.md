Covers cranial nerves, neurotransmitters, autonomic nervous system, action potentials, and brain regions.

## Spinal Cord: "SAME DAVE"
- **S**ensory = **A**fferent, **M**otor = **E**fferent
- **D**orsal = **A**fferent, **V**entral = **E**fferent

## Neurotransmitter Functions
- **Dopa**mine = **Do**ing (reward, motivation, motor function)
- **Sero**tonin = **Serene** (mood, appetite, sleep)
- **GABA** = **G**oing **A**way (main inhibitory NT in CNS)
- **Glut**amate = **Go** (main excitatory NT in CNS)
- **ACh** = **A**ction (neuromuscular junction, parasympathetic)
- **NE** = arousal, alertness, fight-or-flight

---

## Autonomic NS: "Point and Shoot"
- **P**arasympathetic = **P**oint (erection) = "Rest and Digest"
  - Slows heart, constricts pupils, stimulates digestion, constricts bronchioles
  - ACh at both pre- and postganglionic synapses
- **S**ympathetic = **S**hoot (ejaculation) = "Fight or Flight"
  - Increases heart rate, dilates pupils, inhibits digestion, dilates bronchioles
  - ACh preganglionic, NE postganglionic

---

## Action Potential: "Salty Banana"
- At rest (~-70mV): high Na+ outside, high K+ inside (like a salty banana)
- **Depolarization**: Na+ rushes IN (voltage-gated Na+ channels open, peaks ~+35mV)
- **Repolarization**: K+ rushes OUT (Na+ channels inactivate, K+ channels open)
- **Hyperpolarization**: K+ channels close slowly, briefly dips below -70mV
- Na+/K+ ATPase restores gradient: 3 Na+ out, 2 K+ in

---

## Brain Regions: "HATCH"
- **H**ippocampus = memory consolidation (short-term -> long-term declarative)
- **A**mygdala = fear, aggression, emotional processing
- **T**halamus = sensory relay station (all senses EXCEPT smell)
- **C**erebellum = coordination, balance, posture
- **H**ypothalamus = homeostasis (hunger, thirst, temperature, sleep, endocrine control)
- Also: Medulla oblongata = heartbeat, breathing, blood pressure
