# Endocrine System Mnemonics — Kaplan Biology Ch 5

Covers pituitary hormones, adrenal cortex layers, and core endocrine glands.

## Anterior Pituitary Hormones: "FLAT PEG"
- **F**SH, **L**H, **A**CTH, **T**SH = FLAT (tropic hormones -- act on other endocrine glands)
- **P**rolactin, **E**ndorphins, **G**rowth Hormone = PEG (direct hormones -- act on non-endocrine tissues)

## Posterior Pituitary: Only 2
- **ADH** (antidiuretic hormone/vasopressin): water reabsorption in collecting ducts
- **Oxytocin**: uterine contraction, milk let-down
- Made in hypothalamus, stored/released by posterior pituitary

---

## Adrenal Cortex Layers: "GFR = Salt, Sugar, Sex"
- Zona **G**lomerulosa (outer) = **Salt** = mineralocorticoids (aldosterone)
- Zona **F**asciculata (middle) = **Sugar** = glucocorticoids (cortisol)
- Zona **R**eticularis (inner) = **Sex** = androgens (DHEA)
- "The deeper you go, the sweeter it gets"
- Adrenal medulla (separate) = catecholamines (epinephrine, norepinephrine)
