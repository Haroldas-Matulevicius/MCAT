# CARS Mnemonics & Strategies

## Question Categories: "FWB"
- **F**oundation of Comprehension (~30%): main idea, detail, tone, word-in-context
- Reasoning **W**ithin the Text (~30%): integrate parts, evaluate evidence, identify assumptions
- Reasoning **B**eyond the Text (~40%): apply to new situations, strengthen/weaken, predict

---

## Wrong Answer Elimination: "LANE"
- **L**anguage traps: answer uses passage words but distorts the meaning
- **A**bsolutes: "always," "never," "the only reason" -- almost always wrong
- **N**ot supported: relies on outside knowledge or assumptions not in the passage
- **E**xtreme: exaggerates or goes beyond what the author actually claims

---

## Passage Mapping
After each paragraph, assign a 2-3 word mental label (e.g., "author's counterpoint," "historical example," "concession"). Creates a map so you know WHERE to look without re-reading the entire passage.

---

## Question Stem Keywords
Read the question twice. Watch for:
- **EXCEPT** and **NOT** -- flip the question
- **MOST LIKELY** and **IMPLIES** -- signal inference, not direct retrieval
- Missing these qualifiers is one of the most common careless errors
