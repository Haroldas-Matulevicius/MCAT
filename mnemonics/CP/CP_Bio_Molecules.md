# Bio Molecules Mnemonics

## Purines vs. Pyrimidines
- **"CUT the PY"**: Pyrimidines = **C**ytosine, **U**racil, **T**hymine (1 ring)
- **"Pure As Gold"**: Purines = **A**denine, **G**uanine (2 rings)

---

## Disaccharide Compositions: "Super Glowing Frogs Leave Gardens Glowing"
- **S**uper **G**lowing **F**rogs = **S**ucrose = **G**lucose + **F**ructose
- **L**eave **G**ardens **G**lowing = **L**actose = **G**alactose + **G**lucose
- **M**altose = Glucose + Glucose ("malt = double glucose")

---

## Natural Stereochemistry: "LA and DC"
- **L**-Amino acids and **D**-Carbohydrates are the biologically active forms
- Think of Los Angeles (LA) and Washington DC

---

## Beta Anomer: "B UP!!"
- In the beta anomer, the anomeric -OH points UP (axial in Haworth projection)

---

## Sugar Ring Size: "Furanose = Five"
- **F**uranose = **F**ive-membered ring
- Pyranose = six-membered ring

---
