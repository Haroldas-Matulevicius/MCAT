# Molecular Structure & IMFs Mnemonics

## Hydrogen Bonding: "Hold the FON"
- H-bonding requires H bonded to **F**, **O**, or **N**
- These three: most electronegative + small size = strong partial charges

---

## IMF Strength: "I Can Handle Dirty Vans"
- **I**onic > **C**ovalent > **H**ydrogen bonding > **D**ipole-dipole > **V**an der Waals (London dispersion)
- Note: This mixes intramolecular bonds with IMFs. Pure IMF ranking: Ion-dipole > H-bonding > Dipole-dipole > London dispersion

---

## Hybridization Shortcut: Count the Groups
- 2 electron groups = **sp** (linear, 180)
- 3 electron groups = **sp2** (trigonal planar, 120)
- 4 electron groups = **sp3** (tetrahedral, 109.5)
- Number of groups = number of orbitals hybridized = 1 s + (n) p's
