# Thermo, Kinetics & Enzymes Mnemonics

## Gibbs Free Energy: "Goldfish Are Horrible Without Tartar Sauce"
- deltaG = deltaH - T*deltaS
- **G**oldfish = G, **H**orrible = H, **T**artar **S**auce = TS

---

## Heat Transfer: "q = mCAT"
- q = m * c * deltaT
- "You're studying for the mCAT!"
- For heat transfer WITHOUT phase change only

---

## Spontaneity Quick Table
| deltaH | deltaS | Spontaneous? |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Negative | Positive | ALWAYS (deltaG always negative) |
| Positive | Negative | NEVER (deltaG always positive) |
| Negative | Negative | Only at LOW temperature |
| Positive | Positive | Only at HIGH temperature |

---

## Enzyme Inhibitor Effects

| Type | Binds | Km | Vmax |
|------|-------|-----|------|
| **Competitive** | Active site | INCREASES | Unchanged |
| **Noncompetitive** | Allosteric (E or ES equally) | Unchanged | DECREASES |
| **Uncompetitive** | ES complex only | DECREASES | DECREASES |
| **Mixed** | E and ES unequally | INCREASES | DECREASES |

Memory aids:
- "Competitive = Competes at active site" -- more substrate can outcompete, so Vmax is recoverable
- "U in Uncompetitive = parallel lines" on Lineweaver-Burk plot
- "Uncompetitive = Under = binds only ES" (grabs the enzyme-substrate complex)
