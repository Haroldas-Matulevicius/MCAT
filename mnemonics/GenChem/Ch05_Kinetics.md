# Kinetics Mnemonics — Kaplan GChem Ch 5

Covers reaction rates, rate laws, and enzyme inhibition (cross-references Biochem Ch 2).

## Enzyme Inhibitor Effects (cross-load with `mnemonics/Biochemistry/Ch02_Enzymes.md`)

| Type | Binds | Km | Vmax |
|------|-------|-----|------|
| **Competitive** | Active site | INCREASES | Unchanged |
| **Noncompetitive** | Allosteric (E or ES equally) | Unchanged | DECREASES |
| **Uncompetitive** | ES complex only | DECREASES | DECREASES |
| **Mixed** | E and ES unequally | INCREASES | DECREASES |

Memory aids:
- "Competitive = Competes at active site" -- more substrate can outcompete, so Vmax is recoverable
- "U in Uncompetitive = parallel lines" on Lineweaver-Burk plot
- "Uncompetitive = Under = binds only ES" (grabs the enzyme-substrate complex)
