# Gas Phase Mnemonics — Kaplan GChem Ch 8

Covers gas laws and ideal gas behavior.

## Gas Laws
- **Boyle's**: P and V (inverse). "Boys Play Videogames" or "Boyle's = Balloon = Volume"
- **Charles's**: V and T (direct). "Charles in Charge of Volume and Temperature"
- **Gay-Lussac's**: P and T (direct)
- **Ideal Gas Law**: PV = nRT ("piv-nert"). R = 0.0821 L*atm/(mol*K) -- remember "0.08, two-one" (digits 8-2-1)
