# Redox Mnemonics — Kaplan GChem Ch 11

Covers oxidation/reduction identification.

## Oxidation/Reduction: "OIL RIG" or "LEO says GER"
- **O**xidation **I**s **L**oss (of electrons)
- **R**eduction **I**s **G**ain (of electrons)
- **L**ose **E**lectrons = **O**xidation; **G**ain **E**lectrons = **R**eduction
