# Spectroscopy Mnemonics — Kaplan OChem Ch 11

Covers NMR, IR, UV-Vis, and mass spectrometry.

## NMR: "Deshielded = Downfield"
- Protons near electronegative atoms = deshielded = DOWNFIELD (higher ppm, left)
- Shielded protons = UPFIELD (lower ppm, right)
- Both start with D: **D**eshielded = **D**ownfield

---

## NMR Splitting: N+1 Rule
- A proton signal splits into (n+1) peaks
- n = number of equivalent neighboring protons within 3 bonds

---

## Key NMR Chemical Shift Landmarks
- 0-2 ppm: alkyl (CH3, CH2)
- 6-8 ppm: aromatic
- 9-10 ppm: aldehyde
- 10-12 ppm: carboxylic acid

---

## IR: "Two Windows" Approach
- ~3200-3600 cm-1: broad = O-H, sharp = N-H
- ~1700 cm-1 (1650-1750): C=O (carbonyl)
- ~3000 cm-1: C-H stretches (bonus)
- "Look at 3300 for OH/NH, look at 1700 for C=O" -- covers most MCAT IR questions
