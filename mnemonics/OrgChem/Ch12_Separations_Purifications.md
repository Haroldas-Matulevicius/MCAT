Covers extraction, distillation, chromatography, and electrophoresis.

## TLC: "Polar is Lower and Slower"
- On polar stationary phase (silica gel): polar compounds stick, travel less = LOWER Rf
- Nonpolar compounds travel farther = HIGHER Rf
- Rf = distance compound / distance solvent
- Note: reverses in reverse-phase chromatography (MCAT primarily tests normal-phase)

## Size Exclusion Chromatography: "Big Boys Elute First"
- Large molecules excluded from pores -> travel around beads -> elute FIRST
- Small molecules enter pores -> longer path -> elute LATER
- Opposite of gel electrophoresis (where small = faster)

---

## Solubility: "Like Dissolves Like"
- Polar solvents dissolve polar solutes
- Nonpolar solvents dissolve nonpolar solutes
