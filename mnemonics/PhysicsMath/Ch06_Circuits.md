# Circuits Mnemonics — Kaplan Physics & Math Ch 6

Covers Ohm's law, series/parallel circuits, and capacitors.

## Ohm's Law: "VIR Triangle"
- V = IR (cover what you want, remaining letters give the formula)
- I = V/R, R = V/I

---

## Resistors vs. Capacitors: "Opposite Rules"
- Resistors: Series = add directly (R = R1+R2); Parallel = reciprocal (1/R = 1/R1+1/R2)
- Capacitors: OPPOSITE -- Parallel = add directly; Series = reciprocal
- "Whatever rule applies to resistors, flip it for capacitors"
