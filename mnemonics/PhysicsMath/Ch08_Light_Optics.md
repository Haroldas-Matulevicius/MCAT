# Light & Optics Mnemonics — Kaplan Physics & Math Ch 8

Covers EM spectrum, geometric optics (mirrors, lenses), and wave optics.

## EM Spectrum (low to high frequency): "Raging Martians Invaded Venus Using X-ray Guns"
- **R**adio, **M**icrowave, **I**nfrared, **V**isible, **U**ltraviolet, **X**-ray, **G**amma

## Visible Light (low to high frequency): "ROY G. BIV"
- **R**ed, **O**range, **Y**ellow, **G**reen, **B**lue, **I**ndigo, **V**iolet

---

## Optics
- **Real images** are always INVERTED
- **Virtual images** are always UPRIGHT
- Diverging optics produce "**SUV**" images: **S**mall, **U**pright, **V**irtual

### Focal Length Signs: "I'm VEXD that they have -f"
- Con**VEX** mirrors and concave (diverging) lenses = NEGATIVE focal length
- Concave mirrors and convex (converging) lenses = POSITIVE focal length

### Vision Correction: "NMD, FHC"
- **N**ear-sightedness (**M**yopia) = **D**iverging lenses (eyeball too long)
- **F**ar-sightedness (**H**yperopia) = **C**onverging lenses (eyeball too short)
