# Mathematics Mnemonics — Kaplan Physics & Math Ch 10

Covers logarithms, sig figs, metric prefixes, and pH calculations.

## Log Rules: "LAP"
- **L**og of a product = **A**dded logs: log(AB) = log A + log B
- **P**ower rule: log(A^n) = n * log A
- Quotient: log(A/B) = log A - log B

---

## Key Log Value: "Just Memorize log 2 = 0.3"
- log 2 = 0.3 (actual: 0.301)
- Derive the rest:
  - log 4 = log(2^2) = 2(0.3) = 0.6
  - log 5 = log(10/2) = 1 - 0.3 = 0.7
  - log 8 = log(2^3) = 3(0.3) = 0.9
  - log 3 = approximately 0.5

---

## Significant Figures: "Pacific-Atlantic"
- Decimal **P**resent: count from the **P**acific (left) side, starting at first nonzero digit
- Decimal **A**bsent: count from the **A**tlantic (right) side, starting at first nonzero digit
- Addition/subtraction: fewest DECIMAL PLACES
- Multiplication/division: fewest SIG FIGS

## Metric Prefixes: "King Henry Died By Drinking Chocolate Milk"
- **K**ilo (10^3), **H**ecto (10^2), **D**eca (10^1), **B**ase (10^0), **D**eci (10^-1), **C**enti (10^-2), **M**illi (10^-3)
- Extended: "...Maybe Not Pasteurized" = Micro (10^-6), Nano (10^-9), Pico (10^-12)

## pH Quick Rules
- pH + pOH = 14 (at 25C)
- pH = -log[H+]
- Every 10x change in [H+] = 1 unit change in pH
