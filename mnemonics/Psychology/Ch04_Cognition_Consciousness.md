# Cognition & Consciousness Mnemonics — Kaplan Behavioral Sciences Ch 4

Covers Piagetian cognitive development and sleep stages.

## Piaget's Cognitive Development: "Some People Can Fly"
- **S**ensorimotor (0-2): object permanence
- **P**reoperational (2-7): egocentrism, conservation failure
- **C**oncrete operational (7-11): conservation, logical thought about concrete things
- **F**ormal operational (12+): abstract/hypothetical reasoning

---

## Sleep Stage Brain Waves: "BAT-D"
- **B**eta waves: Awake/Alert
- **A**lpha waves: Drowsy/Relaxed
- **T**heta waves: N1-N2 (light sleep; N2 adds sleep spindles + K-complexes)
- **D**elta waves: N3 (deep/slow-wave sleep)
- REM: returns to Beta-like waves (muscle atonia, dreaming)
