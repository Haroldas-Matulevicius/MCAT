# Motivation, Emotion & Stress Mnemonics — Kaplan Behavioral Sciences Ch 5

Covers emotion theories and the General Adaptation Syndrome.

## Emotion Theories: "James, Cannon, Schachter"
- **James-Lange:** stimulus -> body arousal -> emotion (body first)
- **Cannon-Bard:** stimulus -> simultaneous arousal AND emotion (both at once)
- **Schachter-Singer (Two-Factor):** stimulus -> arousal + cognitive label -> emotion (arousal + thinking)

---

## General Adaptation Syndrome: "People with GAS ARE stressed"
- **A**larm: fight-or-flight activation, sympathetic arousal
- **R**esistance: body adapts, maintains elevated metabolic state
- **E**xhaustion: resources depleted, immune suppression, organ damage
