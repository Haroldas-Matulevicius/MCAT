# Identity & Personality Mnemonics — Kaplan Behavioral Sciences Ch 6

Covers Erikson, Freud, Kohlberg developmental stages, and Big Five traits.

## Erikson's 8 Psychosocial Stages: "Trust Autonomy Initiates Industry; Identity Intimacy Generatively Integrates"
1. Trust vs. Mistrust (0-1)
2. Autonomy vs. Shame/Doubt (1-3)
3. Initiative vs. Guilt (3-6)
4. Industry vs. Inferiority (6-12)
5. Identity vs. Role Confusion (12-18)
6. Intimacy vs. Isolation (young adult)
7. Generativity vs. Stagnation (middle adult)
8. Ego Integrity vs. Despair (late adult)

---

## Freud's Psychosexual Stages: "Old Aged People Love Grandchildren"
- **O**ral (0-1)
- **A**nal (1-3)
- **P**hallic (3-6, Oedipus/Electra complex)
- **L**atency (6-12)
- **G**enital (12+)

---

## Kohlberg's Moral Development: "PCP"
- **P**re-conventional: Stage 1 = obedience/punishment, Stage 2 = self-interest
- **C**onventional: Stage 3 = social approval ("good boy/girl"), Stage 4 = law and order
- **P**ost-conventional: Stage 5 = social contract, Stage 6 = universal ethical principles

---

## Big Five Traits: "OCEAN"
- **O**penness (to experience)
- **C**onscientiousness
- **E**xtraversion
- **A**greeableness
- **N**euroticism
