# Social Processes Mnemonics — Kaplan Behavioral Sciences Ch 8

Covers Merton's strain theory and social influence.

## Merton's Strain Theory: "CIRRR"
- **C**onformity: accepts goals + accepts means
- **I**nnovation: accepts goals + rejects means (e.g., crime to get rich)
- **R**itualism: rejects goals + accepts means (going through the motions)
- **R**etreatism: rejects goals + rejects means (drops out)
- **R**ebellion: replaces both goals + means with new ones
