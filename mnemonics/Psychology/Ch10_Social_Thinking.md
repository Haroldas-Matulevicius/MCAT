# Social Thinking Mnemonics — Kaplan Behavioral Sciences Ch 10

Covers attribution biases.

## Three Key Attribution Biases
- **F**undamental **A**ttribution **E**rror: overattribute OTHERS' behavior to internal/dispositional factors
- **S**elf-serving bias: attribute OWN success to internal, OWN failure to external
- **A**ctor-observer bias: attribute OWN behavior to situation, OTHERS' to disposition
