## Cross-loading Rules

- **Lab techniques overlap** (chromatography applied to biomolecules, electrophoresis): also load `research/Lab_Techniques.md`.
- **Research methods or statistics**: also load `research/Research_Methods.md`.
- **GC ↔ OC bonding overlap:** atomic orbitals/quantum numbers appear in both `CP_GC_Ch01` and `CP_OC_Ch03`. Load both for hybridization questions.
- **Enzyme kinetics ↔ biochem:** for enzyme kinetics questions, load `CP_GC_Ch05_Kinetics.md` AND `research/BB/BB_Ch02_Enzymes.md`.
- **Biomolecule chemistry ↔ biochem:** for AAs/peptides/proteins/sugars/lipids, load `CP_OC_Ch10_Nitrogen_Phosphorus.md` (synthesis) AND the relevant `BB_Ch01...Ch05` (structure/function).
