## Cross-loading Rules

- If the topic involves **lab techniques** (chromatography, spectroscopy applications, centrifugation), also load `research/Lab_Techniques.md`.
- If the topic involves **research methods or statistics**, also load `research/Research_Methods.md`.
- For **biochemistry overlap** (enzyme kinetics, biomolecules), consider also loading the relevant `research/BB/` file.
